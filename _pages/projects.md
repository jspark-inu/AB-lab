## Research Interests

The **Algorithm & Behavior Lab** focuses on the intersection of human psychology and artificial intelligence.

### 1. Human-AI Collaboration
We explore the dynamics of how humans and AI work together, identifying friction points and designing systems that enhance collaborative intelligence.
- **Key Topics**: Algorithmic Friction, Human-in-the-loop, AI Acceptance

### 2. Computational Behavioral Science
Utilizing large-scale data and computational methods to understand human decision-making processes in digital environments.
- **Key Topics**: Digital Nudging, Symbolic Adoption, User Behavior Analysis
